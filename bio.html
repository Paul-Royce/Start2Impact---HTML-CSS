MY PATH TO PROGRAMMING

My name is Paul i am 23 years old, i was born in Plaermo (Italy) but currently i live in Milan.
My journey into programming has his roots in my childhood. In fact, since i was a kid i was fascinated about complexity, especially when it had to do with the means of transport.
Growing up my love for complexity has grown even more when i fell in love with the airplanes systems (autopilot, automated safety measures, ecc), and all of this made me think that i should have become an airline pilot, not because i was interested in flying itself, but just for the sake of dealing with all those systems.

Depite this dream, things in my personal life have not turned out the way i hoped, making really difficult for me to archieve this goal. At the time i got really confused and angry ,but not all evil comes to harm. This state of confusion led me to a self development path, where i discovered other interests and passions such as: economy, finance, marketing, philosophy, investments and.....learning.

Especially the last one was a surprise because I didn't get along with it very well at high school. I guess it is due to the fact that in schools (at least the Italian ones) studying and learning are seen as an obligation, rather than as a way of feeding and satisfying curiosity at the same time, as well as a form of entertainment (at least to me).

Above all these new interests, and despite not being able to become an airline pilot, for a variety of reasons, there was always a willingness of mine to deal with the complex, so after a period of time in which I explored the world of UX Design and Digital marketing thanks to start2impact, I finally landed in programming, where I re-discovered my passion for the complex and automatisms.
